Introduction


My name is Ethan Kilpatrick. I am a senior computer engineering student at Mississippi State University. This website serves the purpose of completing an Embedded System dissection for ECE 4723 - Embedded Systems in the Spring of 2023.

The focus of this website is to detail the reverse engineering of the handheld grill thermometer created by ExpertGrill shown in the picture to the left. A detailed overview and analysis of the product lies ahead.

Product Market and Retail Information


This pocket thermometer was developed by a company called Expert Grill, which is a brand developed by and for Walmart. All Expert Grill products’ manufacturing is outsourced by Walmart to a company called NexGrill, which is also responsible for manufacturing grills for Costco, Home Depot, and Lowes.
